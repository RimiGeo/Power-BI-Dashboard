# Power-BI-Dashboard
Specific to the product and period in the data
This repository contains the data, analysis, and interactive dashboard files for a comprehensive **Sales Performance Review** covering **Q1-Q3 of the year 2022**.

The project aims to analyze sales trends across various geographies and product lines, identify top performers, and provide actionable insights for strategic decision-making in Q4.

## üîç Key Findings (As seen in the BI Visuals)

The analysis was driven by the following key insights extracted from the data:

* **Geographical Performance:** The UK and India are the dominant sales territories, while the USA and New Zealand represent the largest opportunities for improvement.
* **Product Performance:** Sales are heavily skewed towards high-volume products like **Peanut Butter Cubes**.
* **Individual Performance:** Sales results show a clear top-tier of performers who could potentially lead training initiatives.
